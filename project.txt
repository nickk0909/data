PROJECT TITLE :- Jobs Portal

DISTRIBUTION :-Jobs portal is developed so that candidates can find job vacancy and Employers can post jobs. On the whole the objective of the project is to enable job seekers to place their resumes and employers to publish their jobs. It enables job seekers to post their resume, search for jobs. It will provide various companies to post their job on the site. Apart from this there will be an admin module for the admin to make changes to the database content.
